# AeroBooks Privacy Policy Last updated: March 28, 2026 ## Overview AeroBooks is a reading tracker for managing your library, reviews, reading progress, streaks, reminders, and optional account features. This Privacy Policy explains what data AeroBooks processes, why it is processed, and which third-party services may be involved. ## Data AeroBooks Processes Depending on how you use the app, AeroBooks may process: - profile information such as your first name and birthday - account information when you choose Sign in with Apple - reading data such as saved books, reading status, progress history, reviews, ratings, series, streaks, and streak freezes - reminder preferences such as notification status and reminder time - backup data stored by you in iCloud Drive when iCloud backup is enabled ## Where Data Is Stored Most AeroBooks data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud backup, backup files are stored in your iCloud container so you can restore them later on your own devices. ## Sign in with Apple and Firebase Authentication If you use Sign in with Apple, AeroBooks uses Firebase Authentication to complete and manage sign-in. This may involve processing account-related identifiers such as: - your Apple sign-in credential - an authentication token - a Firebase user identifier - your name or email address if provided by Apple during sign-in Firebase Authentication is provided by Google. You should also review: - Google Firebase Privacy and Security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy - Apple support resources: https://support.apple.com/ ## Notifications If you allow notifications, AeroBooks can send local reminders to help protect your reading streak. Notification preferences are stored on your device. AeroBooks does not use notifications for advertising. ## iCloud Backup If you enable iCloud backup, AeroBooks can create backup files containing your reading data and profile data. These files are stored in your iCloud storage and are controlled through your Apple account. ## Data Sharing AeroBooks does not sell your personal data. Your data is only shared with third parties when necessary to provide functionality you choose to use, such as: - Firebase Authentication for account sign-in - Apple iCloud services for backup storage ## Your Choices You can: - use the app as a guest without Sign in with Apple - disable streak reminders - disable iCloud backup - reset the app and remove locally stored data from within the app You can also manage Sign in with Apple and iCloud settings through your Apple account settings. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: - Name: Enzo Kaiser - Email: YOUR-SUPPORT-EMAIL@example.com - Support page: https://support.apple.com/ ## Replace Before Release Before publishing this policy, replace: - `YOUR-SUPPORT-EMAIL@example.com` - the placeholder support link - any wording that does not match your final app behavior
