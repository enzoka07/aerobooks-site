Help Topics

Sign in with Apple If Sign in with Apple does not work, make sure your internet connection is active, restart the app, and confirm that Sign in with Apple is available on your device.
Notifications If streak reminders do not arrive, open iPhone Settings, choose AeroBooks, allow notifications, and check that the reminder time is enabled in AeroBooks settings.
iCloud Backup If backup or restore does not work, make sure iCloud Drive is enabled, confirm that you are signed into the correct Apple ID, and check whether AeroBooks has iCloud access.
Reading Data If covers, progress, or reading history look incorrect, reopen the book detail view, check whether the book is still in your reading list, and verify the latest progress entry in the history list.

Response Time
