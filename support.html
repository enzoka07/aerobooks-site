# AeroBooks Support Welcome to AeroBooks Support. ## Contact If you need help, contact: - Name: Enzo Kaiser - Email: hello.aerostudios@gmail.com ## Help Topics ### Sign in with Apple If Sign in with Apple does not work: - make sure your internet connection is active - try again after restarting the app - check whether Sign in with Apple is available on your device ### Notifications If streak reminders do not arrive: - open iPhone Settings - choose AeroBooks - allow notifications - check that the reminder time is enabled in AeroBooks settings ### iCloud Backup If backup or restore does not work: - make sure iCloud Drive is enabled on your device - make sure you are signed into the correct Apple ID - confirm that AeroBooks has iCloud access ### Reading Data If covers, progress, or reading history look incorrect: - reopen the book detail view - check whether the book is still in your reading list - verify the latest progress entry in the history list ## Response Time Support requests are usually answered as soon as possible.
